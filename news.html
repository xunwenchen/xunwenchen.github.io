2025

  • 14th Mar 2025. All book chapter titles fixed for "Handbook of Sustainable Management of Biodiversity: Strategies for a Thriving Planet". Thank all contributors.
  • 2nd Jan 2025. The proposal of the new book "Sustainable Management of Biodiversity: Strategies for a Thriving Planet" has been approved by CRC Press/Taylor & Francis. Editors: Ming Hung Wong, Xun Wen Chen, and Masayuki Ushio. We are welcoming chapter contributions!

2024

  • 24 Dec. 2024. I am happy to announce that we are initiating a new book "Sustainable Management of Biodiversity". Editors: Ming Hung Wong, Xun Wen Chen, and Masayuki Ushio.

    Please contact us if you would like to contribute a book chapter.


  • 16-18 Dec. 2024. The 1st CHEER2024 and the 10th WRE conferences successfully held.

  • Oct. 2024. A collaborative work (with HKUST) on mycorrhizal compacted soils published in Plant and Soil

  • Aug. 2024. A new project working on soil microbial assembly funded by NSFC
